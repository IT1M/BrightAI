What is RPA?

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) refers to software bots that can perform routine tasks instead of employees. Imagine a digital employee working 24/7 without fatigue, errors, and at lightning speed.

The bot can copy, paste, open apps, browse websites, read emails, fill government forms, and update databases - all completely automatically with zero human intervention!

FAQ

Will RPA replace my job?

Never. RPA takes the "boring work" and repetitive tasks, giving employees a chance to focus on tasks that require thinking, creativity, and human connection. It's an assistant, not a replacement.
